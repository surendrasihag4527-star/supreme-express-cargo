Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Part Load Transport Service (PTL)? expand_more

Part Load Transport (also known as Part Truck Load or PTL) is a logistics method where a single truck carries shipments from multiple clients. Each customer pays only for the volume or weight of the space their cargo occupies, making it an extremely cost-efficient option for smaller corporate payloads that do not require an entire vehicle.

How is PTL pricing calculated? expand_more

PTL pricing is calculated based on either the actual weight or the volumetric weight of your shipment (whichever is higher), along with the distance of the transit route. Supreme Express Cargo uses transparent dimension weight matrices to offer very competitive and fair rates.

Do you offer tracking for PTL consignments? expand_more

Yes, absolutely. Every PTL consignment with Supreme Express Cargo receives a unique tracking ID. Customers can monitor their shipment status in real-time through our integrated digital platform as it moves across our nationwide hub networks.
